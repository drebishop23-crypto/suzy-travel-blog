My Story

From Lisbon to Everywhere

I'm Suzy — a travel blogger, amateur photographer, and full-time wanderer originally from the sun-soaked streets of Lisbon, Portugal. What started as a solo weekend trip to Morocco two years ago somehow turned into a life lived mostly out of a single carry-on bag. I started this blog to remember every meal, every wrong turn, and every breathtaking view that made me feel completely, wonderfully lost.

My travel style is slow and curious — I'd rather spend three weeks in one country than rush through five. I'm drawn to local markets, hole-in-the-wall restaurants, long train journeys, and the kind of conversations that only happen when you're far from home. Whether you're planning your first solo trip or your fiftieth adventure, I hope something on this blog makes you feel brave enough to book the ticket.
